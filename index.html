About

Danielle Zacharias

VDN works at the intersection of technical drafting and interior design. Danielle's background is in architectural technology — she produces construction documents in Revit and AutoCAD, specification schedules, and project visualizations. She also sources furniture, art, and materials with a sharp eye rooted in design history, not trends.

From a single-room refresh to a 38-unit multifamily build, every project starts the same way: listening. The technical precision of drafting meets a curious, opinionated eye for interiors — and the result is a space that genuinely belongs to the people in it.

Services

What we do

01

Design Consultation

We study the conditions of your space — light, layout, how you actually live in it — and build a design strategy grounded in your goals. From a single room to a ground-up build.

02

Technical Drawing & CAD

Detailed technical drawings, space planning, and CAD documentation in Revit, AutoCAD, and Inventor. Precision that supports the design vision.

03

Project Visualization

Renderings and visual presentations that communicate design intent clearly — helping clients see and feel the space before a single element is placed.

04

Material & Finish Selection

Materials, finishes, and palettes that serve both the vision and the budget. We source, sample, and document everything in specification sheets your contractor can build from.

05

Space Curation

Selecting and sourcing furnishings, materials, art, and objects that create a cohesive narrative. Not decoration — curation with intention and context.

06

Decor & Styling

The final layer that brings a space to life. Thoughtful placement of objects, textiles, and art that transforms rooms into environments that feel collected over time.

Philosophy
Good design isn't just seen — it's felt. It grounds you, stirs you, and leaves its mark long after you've moved on.
