About

Solving spaces

Every space has its own set of challenges — the light, the layout, the way people actually move through a room. We study those conditions and work within them, drawing on deep roots in design history to find solutions that feel both refined and lived-in.

Danielle approaches each project as a problem solver and a conduit — translating the personality and tastes of the people in the home into something tangible. The technical precision of drafting meets a sharp, curious eye for interiors, and the result is a space that genuinely belongs to the people in it.

Services

What we do

01

Design Consultation

Comprehensive design direction for residential and commercial spaces. From concept through completion, we guide every aesthetic and functional decision.

02

Technical Drawing & CAD

Detailed technical drawings, space planning, and CAD documentation in Revit, AutoCAD, and Inventor. Precision that supports the design vision.

03

Project Visualization

Renderings and visual presentations that communicate design intent clearly — helping clients see and feel the space before a single element is placed.

04

Material & Finish Selection

Expert guidance on materials, finishes, and palettes. We source options that serve both the aesthetic vision and practical requirements of every project.

05

Space Curation

Selecting and sourcing furnishings, materials, art, and objects that create a cohesive narrative. Not decoration — curation with intention and context.

06

Decor & Styling

The final layer that brings a space to life. Thoughtful placement of objects, textiles, and art that transforms rooms into environments that feel collected over time.

Philosophy
We are problem solvers rooted in design history. Every project is a unique set of constraints, tastes, and possibilities — and we treat it that way.
